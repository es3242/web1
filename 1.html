GUACAMOLE

This is how you make a great Guacamole Watch and Learn!

Ingredients

  1. One Avocado

  2. Quater of Chopped Onion

  3. Half of Chopped Tomato

  4. One table spoon of Saults

  5. Little bit of peppers

  6. Lime juice

  7. Silantro

Method

1.Cut the avocado in half and take the seed out. Scratch the magin with a spoon tring to take the all green avocado out of the peels

2.Smash the green avocado with a smasher with mutiple holes on it and put the chopped onions, tomatoes, Saults, Peppers, Lime juice, Silantro.

3.Mix all with a spoon.
