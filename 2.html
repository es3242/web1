BURRITO

This is how you make a homemade burrito Watch and Learn!

Ingredients

  1. Tortilla
    -105gms of flour
    -2gms of baking powder
    -1gm of salt
    -15gms of butter
    -75ml of water

  2. Chicken Filling
    - Oil
    - Chopped Onion
    - Crushed garlic
    - Cubes of Chicken
    - Soaked black beans
    - Hot Salsa
    - Cayenne Pepper
    - Salt
    - Crushed black pepper corns

  3. Assemble
    - Salad Cream
    - Left over rice mixed with coriander & paprika
    - cheese
    - coriander leaves

Method

  1. Make dough by adding flour, baking powder, salt, butter mix it well and then add water.

  2. On a flat surface/silicon mat, roll the dough balls to form tortilla.

  3. Keep a pan on medium flame and heat the Tortilla till its golden brown on one side. Flip it and cook in on the other side.

  4. Heat some oil on a medium flame, add chopped onions and crushed garlic cook until they turn translucent

  5. Add cubes of chicken cook it till the chicken is no longer pink add more oil if needed

  6. Add soaked black beans let them cook.

  7. On low heat add hot salsa, cayenne pepper, salt and black pepper
